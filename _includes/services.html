Digital Strategy

Before you worry about technology stacks, you need to be clear about your project goals and your definition of success. I'll help you focus on the problems you're trying to solve, rather than choosing the first technical solutions that jump to mind. In addition, I balance the needs of user and business with the available technology. I love helping organziations and individuals find the right strategy to address all of the above.

User-Centered, Data-Driven Design

Sometimes it's hard to hear honest feedback from the people you think you're serving -- buts it's essential that you understand what your users want, and plan from their perspective. Let's find the best ways to do that. And while we're at it, let's make sure your readers are really clicking that button as much as you think they are, and can read that beautiful font.
