Strategize

Before you worry about technology stacks, you need to be clear about your project goals and your definition of success. We'll focus on the problems you're trying to solve, rather than choosing technical solutions first.

Promote Best Practices

To achieve your digital goals, you need to allow your design to be data-driven, and your development to be user-centered. Sometimes it's hard to hear honest feedback from the people you think you're serving -- but it's essential! While you're at it, you'll want to make sure readers are clicking that beautifully designed button, and can read that awesome font.
